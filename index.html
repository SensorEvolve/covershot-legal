Covershot — Support

Covershot is an AI photography app for iPhone, iPad and Mac: add a few reference photos of a person once, direct a shot with visual controls — no prompts — and get consistent portraits of the same character.

Contact

Questions, problems, or reports about content: email carljohan.legert@gmail.com. We aim to reply within a few business days.

You can also report any render directly in the app (flag button on the render's detail page) and manage your data under Info → Privacy & Data — including withdrawing AI consent, deleting all renders, or deleting your account and all data.

Membership & credits

1 credit = 1 generated image; failed or moderation-blocked images are refunded automatically. The monthly membership is managed through your Apple ID subscriptions (in-app: Manage Membership).
